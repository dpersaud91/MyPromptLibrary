# Research Prompts

## Explain a Topic
Explain the following topic clearly. Start with a plain English summary,
then go deeper. Assume I have no background knowledge:
Topic: [topic]

## Research and Summarize
Research the following topic and give me:
1. A clear explanation of what it is
2. Why it matters
3. Key facts, data, or findings
4. Gaps or things still unknown
5. Your overall conclusion
Topic: [topic]

## Compare Options
Compare the following options across these criteria: [list criteria].
Present as a structured comparison and end with a clear recommendation
and reasoning:
Options: [list options]

## Summarize My Notes
Summarize the following notes into a clear structured report with sections
for overview, key findings, and recommended next steps:
[paste notes]

## Find Weaknesses in an Argument
Analyze the following argument or claim. What evidence supports it? What
evidence contradicts it? How strong is the overall case?
Argument: [paste]

## Literature Overview
Give me an overview of what is currently known about the following topic.
Cover the main schools of thought, key debates, and most important
findings:
Topic: [topic]
