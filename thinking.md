# Thinking Prompts

## Brainstorm Ideas

Topic: [topic]

## Make a Decision
Help me make a decision. Ask me up to 5 clarifying questions first, then
help me think through it systematically using a clear framework:
Decision I am facing: [describe]
Options I am considering: [list]

## Challenge My Thinking
I am going to share an idea or plan. Play devil's advocate and challenge
my assumptions. What am I missing? What could go wrong? What would a
smart critic say?
My idea: [describe]

## First Principles
Break down the following problem or idea using first principles thinking.
Strip away assumptions and rebuild the reasoning from the ground up:
Topic: [describe]

## Think Out Loud With Me
I want to think through something out loud. Ask me questions to help me
clarify my thinking. Do not give me answers — just ask good questions
that push my thinking further:
Topic: [describe]

## Identify Blind Spots
Review the following plan or argument. What assumptions am I making that
could be wrong? What perspectives or risks am I not considering?
[paste plan or argument]

and consider both short-term and long-term implications:
